The culture of design

Kasa Atelier is a multidisciplinary firm specializing in space planning, design conceptualization and execution envisaged by two budding designers kahan patil & jason albuquerque who came along to study the art of Interior Design in Mumbai, India and thereafter decided to pursue a master’s degree in Florence, Italy which furthermore fueled their passion for all things art, architecture & design and nurtured a vision of starting their own practice.
At Kasa, we aim at creating a social impact through a blend of innovative problem-solving solutions which are aesthetically pleasing, detail oriented and an out of the box vision by blending age-old techniques and crafts in innovative ways to create a modern outcome that appeals to a global palette.
